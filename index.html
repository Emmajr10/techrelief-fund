Support IT Access for Everyone

The purpose of this fundraising initiative is to provide free IT support services, including computer repair, troubleshooting, and system setup for individuals who lack access to professional assistance. Funds will also be used to refurbish and distribute laptops to students who cannot afford personal devices. In addition, the initiative will deliver basic cybersecurity services, such as network configuration and malware protection, to ensure safe and secure digital participation for families and small businesses..
