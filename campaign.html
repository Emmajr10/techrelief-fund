Meet Amin, a high school junior who dreams of becoming a software engineer. Before receiving a refurbished laptop, she completed assignments using her phone and borrowed devices from classmates. With reliable access to her own computer, Amina can now attend virtual tutoring sessions, complete coding projects, and apply for college scholarships confidently.

This campaign collects donated laptops, professionally refurbishes them, installs updated software, and distributes them to underserved students who lack access to personal technology.
