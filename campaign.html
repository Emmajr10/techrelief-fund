Laptops for Students

Funding Goal: $5,000

Amount Raised: $3,750 (75%)

Many students in underserved communities do not have access to a personal computer at home. This campaign collects donated laptops, professionally refurbishes them, and distributes them to students who rely on shared devices or smartphones for schoolwork.

One student, Amina, previously completed assignments using only her phone. After receiving a refurbished laptop, she now participates in virtual tutoring, completes research projects efficiently, and is preparing for college applications.

Cybersecurity for Small Businesses

Funding Goal: $8,000

Amount Raised: $4,200 (52%)

Small businesses are increasingly targeted by cyberattacks but often cannot afford professional IT protection. This campaign funds basic cybersecurity services including firewall setup, antivirus installation, and secure network configuration.

By supporting this initiative, donors help local businesses protect customer data, prevent ransomware attacks, and operate safely in the digital economy.
